Matcha The Mate

Our goal is to provide a healthy alternative to coffee

Only the highest quality matcha and yerba mate items are offered by Matcha The Mate online store. Our products are a natural form of caffeine that provide you a boost to feel more alert and focus better without giving you the crash that traditional coffee does.

Try our Matcha's grassy sweetness taste and Yerba Mate's smoky and woody taste.





Yerba mate is an infusion made from holly tree leaves and sticks, and matcha is a fine powder made from tea plant leaves. Although they have different flavor characteristics and nutritional profiles, both provide a wholesome, down-to-earth experience in every cup and product.
